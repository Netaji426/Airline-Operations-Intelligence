# Airline Operations Intelligence & Disruption Risk Analysis

Performance intelligence platform analyzing more than 500,000 commercial flight records to identify operational bottlenecks, disruption drivers, airport vulnerabilities, and network resilience patterns using SQL, Power BI, Excel, and AI-assisted exploratory analytics.

## Business Problem

Airlines operate within highly interconnected networks where a disruption at a single airport, route, or operational process can cascade across the entire system. Traditional airline performance metrics often focus on delays and cancellations after they occur, providing limited insight into the underlying drivers of operational disruption.

This project was designed to answer a central question:

**If you became the COO of an airline tomorrow, where should you focus first to reduce disruptions, improve operational efficiency, and increase network resilience?**

The objective was to transform raw operational data into a decision-support system capable of identifying controllable operational failures, high-risk airports, vulnerable routes, and the factors most responsible for network disruption.

## Key Business Questions

* Which airports act as the largest operational bottlenecks?
* Which airlines demonstrate the strongest operational resilience?
* What factors contribute most significantly to delays?
* Which disruptions create the largest downstream network impact?
* How effectively do airlines recover from operational disruptions?
* Which routes carry the highest operational risk?
* What percentage of delays are controllable by airlines?
* Which airports are most vulnerable to cascading disruptions?
* How does increasing traffic volume affect network performance?
* Where should operational investments be prioritized for maximum impact?
